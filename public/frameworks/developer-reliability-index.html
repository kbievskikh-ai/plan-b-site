Developer Reliability Index™

Every Santa Catarina developer scored on a letter-grade scale from A+ to D, based on delivery history, financial health, customer satisfaction, and legal compliance. Updated quarterly.

What We Measure

📋 Delivery History

Percentage of projects delivered on time. Track record of delays. Quality of completed properties vs. marketing promises.

💰 Financial Health

SPE capitalization level. Patrimônio de afetação status. Developer equity contribution. Bank financing relationships.

⭐ Customer Satisfaction

Post-delivery reviews. Warranty claim handling. Responsiveness to buyer concerns. RECLAME AQUI rating.

⚖️ Legal Compliance

CRECI registration status. Pending lawsuits. Cartório disputes. Contract transparency score.

How We Collect Data

We verify every developer through:

  • Junta Comercial SPE registration and capitalization records
  • Cartório de Registro de Imóveis for property history and liens
  • CRECI-SC licensing and disciplinary records
  • RECLAME AQUI consumer complaint database
  • Post-delivery site visits to completed projects
  • Interviews with past buyers (anonymized)
